GrowthHub

A self-improvement app that creates a more personalized experience for different users.

Problem

Numerous self-improvement applications focus on aspects like goal setting and meditation, but they often lack a sense of community that can offer solace to individuals who may experience feelings of loneliness during their journey of personal growth.

Solution

Develop a platform dedicated to self-improvement, providing a vibrant hub where individuals on their personal growth journey can connect with one another in real-time, share experiences, exchange tips, and feel a stronger sense of community.

Research

Competitor Analysis

Competitor analysis was conducted to understand key features and issues from similar self-improvement apps, Headspace and Clarity. This helped identify what users were looking for in an app, whether itâ€™s UI changes or new features.

Guerilla Research

User reviews from the App Store provided insights into user concerns about UI and features, offering a baseline understanding of pain points and guiding the app's structure and Information Architecture development.

Research Takeaways

User Persona

Findings were summarized into user personas, identifying common pain points and needs for stakeholders of this app.

Ideating

Task Flow / User Flow

Task flow mapping helped identify possible screens and steps users would need to take to complete tasks, aiding the development of the final user flow document.

Low-fidelity Wireframes

Low-fidelity Wireframes were created to outline the overall layout of the app, setting the foundation for High-fidelity Wireframes.

Final Designs

Brand Identity

A clean and simple UI was chosen, using an analogous color scheme with blues representing trust and greens symbolizing growth and peace. Consistency in color and design elements was maintained to provide a cohesive user experience.

High-fidelity Wireframes

The High-fidelity Wireframes focused on visual hierarchy, accessibility, and enhancing the overall user experience.

User Feedback

A/B Testing

A/B Testing with potential users provided feedback that enhanced user accessibility and usability, leading to a more impactful user experience.
